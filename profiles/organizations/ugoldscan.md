# UGOLDscan Organization

UGOLDscan is maintained within the UGOLD ecosystem to provide open and auditable blockchain exploration infrastructure.

## Mission

Deliver a public, user-focused explorer with transparent RPC behavior, privacy-minded defaults, and strong support for Ethereum and BSC.
